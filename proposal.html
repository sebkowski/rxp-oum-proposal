01 — Overview

An Immersive Setting for
Visionary Discourse

Building on the success of the 2025 series, the 2026 Public Lecture Series moves into a space that embodies the "Digital Future." RXP @ REXKL provides a fully immersive, high-tech environment — 360-degree projection mapping, architectural-scale visuals, and spatial audio — that transforms a lecture into an experience.

Our proposal focuses on spatial transformation, premium hospitality for VVIPs, and seamless flow for a boutique audience of 100–150 participants.

02 — The Space

Where Technology
Meets Storytelling

RXP Hall transforms for every event. 18 laser projectors, media servers, and a professional sound system create a fully immersive environment. The space shifts from lecture theatre to 360-degree immersive canvas within minutes.

Lecture and panel configuration — MDEC Immersive KL (August 2024)

03 — Spatial Design

Lecture Configuration

The hall will be configured for a premium lecture experience with clear stage visibility, dedicated media zones, and seamless VVIP flow.

Stage & Presentation

Raised stage with projection-mapped backdrop. Custom OUM/CENDEF visuals across 360° surfaces. Speakers illuminated with dedicated wash lights and spotlights.

Seating Layout

100–150 premium chairs in lecture rows with wide spacing. Networking stools at the rear. Bean bag lounge for casual breakout.

Media & Interview Area

Two options — foyer (high visibility) or prep area (controlled, quiet). Equipped for press interactions with branded backdrop.

VVIP Flow

Minister and Global Fellows enter via Artist Room behind stage. Private holding area with premium sofas, direct stage access.

05 — Technical

Immersive Production Suite

RXP's permanent infrastructure eliminates external AV rental. The space is designed ground-up for immersive projection and spatial audio.

Digital Canvas

18 laser projectors delivering 360° projection mapping across walls and floor. Custom OUM-branded visuals for each speaker segment.

Audio System

Wireless microphones (lapel + handheld). Surround sound for spatial audio. Fully integrated with media servers.

Stage Lighting

Wash lights and spotlights ensure speakers are visible against the immersive backdrop. Atmospheric lighting synchronised with visual content.

Climate Control

Supplementary air conditioning for guest comfort in an enclosed 150-pax immersive projection environment.

Accessibility: REXKL is a heritage building with stairs-only access to upper floors. All invitations should include a disclaimer. VVIPs can be dropped off at the main entrance.

06 — Access & Parking

Getting Here

Parking Options

  • Lorong Sultan covered car park — 2-minute walk to REXKL. Sheltered parking, recommended for all guests.
  • Jalan Sultan lots — nearby mall and open-air facilities within walking distance.
  • VVIP drop-off — vehicles can drop off directly at the REXKL main entrance on Jalan Sultan.

A detailed parking guide with map will be included in all guest invitations.
