01 — Overview

An Immersive Setting for
Visionary Discourse

Building on the success of the 2025 series, the 2026 Public Lecture Series moves into a space that embodies the "Digital Future." RXP @ REXKL provides a fully immersive, high-tech environment — 360-degree projection mapping, architectural-scale visuals, and spatial audio — that transforms a lecture into an experience.

This proposal accommodates approximately 230 attendees — 100 external guests (including 50 VIPs), 80 participants, and 50 OUM staff — with a full-day programme from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, premium VVIP hospitality, and three catered meal breaks.

05 — Technical

Immersive Production Suite

RXP's permanent infrastructure eliminates external AV rental. The space is designed ground-up for immersive projection and spatial audio.

Digital Canvas

18 laser projectors delivering 360° projection mapping across walls and floor. Custom OUM-branded visuals for each speaker segment.

Audio System

Wireless microphones (lapel + handheld). Surround sound for spatial audio. Fully integrated with media servers.

Stage Lighting

Wash lights and spotlights ensure speakers are visible against the immersive backdrop. Atmospheric lighting synchronised with visual content.

Climate Control

Supplementary air conditioning for guest comfort in an enclosed 150-pax immersive projection environment.

Accessibility: REXKL is a heritage building with stairs-only access to upper floors. All invitations should include a disclaimer. VVIPs can be dropped off at the main entrance.
